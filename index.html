WEAKPASS

Generate wordlist

Here you can generate a wordlist based on specific input data. For example, by entering an Acme.corp you will receive a list of possible passwords like Acme.corp2018!, Acme.corp123, and so on. All data is processed on the client with JavaScript.

You can use hashcat rules to generate a wordlist. By default, the generator will use its own rules, which you can find here or click on "Show rules".
